Seattle Vert Ramp: Schedule

The goal is to have the ramp built and ready to skate as soon as possible. The more people who volunteer to help, the faster this will happen! If you're interested in helping, email wstein@gmail.com (or message William Stein on facebook) for more information. Also, join the Seattle Vertramp Facebook page.
