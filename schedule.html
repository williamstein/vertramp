Seattle Vert Ramp: Schedule

The goal is to have the ramp built and ready to skate as soon as possible. The more people who volunteer to help, the faster this will happen! If you're interested in helping, email wstein@gmail.com (or message William Stein on facebook) for more information. Also, join the Seattle Vertramp Facebook page.
  • August 2012: Excavation and concrete foundations (done)
  • Friday, Sept 7: First wood drop (67 sheets of 3/4 inch plywood and over 6,760 feet of 2x6's, and 672 feet of 4x6's)
  • 8am Saturday Sept 8 - 11am Sunday, Sept 9: Build the flat bottom
  • 11am Sunday, Sept 9 - 6pm Sunday, Sept 9: Build the first complete 8' wide by 13.5' tall framed transition section
  • Tentative Goal by Sunday, Sept 16: Finish framing the east side of the ramp.
  • Tentative Goal by Sunday, Sept 23: Finish framing the west side of the ramp.
  • TBA: Second wood drop -- massive amount of 3/8 plywood and more 2x6's
  • Sept 30?: First layer of plywood; frame decks
  • Very tentative Goal by October 7: Second layer of plywood
  • TBA: Third wood drop -- 95 sheets of birch
  • Very, very tentative goal: October 21: Final layer of birch
  • October 31: Halloween Skate Jam!?
