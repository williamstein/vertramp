The Seattle Vert Ramp

The Seatle Vert Ramp is a private modern indoor contest-spec skateboard ramp that is 13.5' tall, 56' wide, has 14' of flat, a birch riding surface, has lights, and is covered by a tension fabric roof. It's a training facility for the next crop of up-and-coming Seattle-area professional vert skateboarders, as well as many other skaters who just love to ride big transitions.
