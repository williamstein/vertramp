Seattle Vert Ramp

     

The Seatle Vert Ramp will be a private modern indoor contest-spec skateboard ramp that will be 13.5' tall and 56' wide, with 14' of flat and a birch surface. It will be a training facility for the next crop of up-and-coming Seattle-area professional vert skateboarders, as well as many older skaters who love to ride big transitions.
